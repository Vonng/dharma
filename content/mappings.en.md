This page is the crossroads of all seven volumes. In the final version it will extract stable mappings between religious concepts and engineering concepts from every volume, browsable by either "religious tradition" or "AI concept." For now, a static table holds the slot — nailing down structure and layout first.

No need to over-engineer on day one. v1 just needs to carry the mapping table, stay readable on mobile, and scan well on desktop. That clears the bar for an early ship.
